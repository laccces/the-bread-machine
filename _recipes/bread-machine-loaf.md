## Ingredients
- 270 ml water  
- 15 ml olive oil  
- 2 tsp Demerara sugar (8 g)  
- 11 g salt  
- 365 g strong white bread flour  
- 7 g instant yeast   

## Method

**Step 1**  
Add ingredients to the bread pan in this order, unless your machine specifies otherwise: water → oil → sugar → salt → flour → yeast

**Step 2**  
Select the basic / white programme.

**Step 3**  
Choose the 750g loaf size.

**Step 4**  
Set crust to dark / crusty.

**Step 5**  
Remove the loaf promptly once baked and cool fully on a wire rack.

**Step 6**  
For extra crust, place directly on an oven rack at 180°C for 8–10 minutes.

## Ingredients
- 270 ml water  
- 15 ml olive oil  
- 2 tsp Demerara sugar (8 g)  
- 11 g salt  
- 365 g strong white bread flour  
- 7 g instant yeast   

## Method
1. Add ingredients to the bread pan in this order, unless your machine specifies otherwise:  
   water → oil → sugar → salt → flour → yeast  
2. Select the basic / white programme.  
3. Choose the 750g loaf size.  
4. Set crust to dark / crusty.  
5. Remove the loaf promptly once baked and cool fully on a wire rack.  
6. For extra crust, place directly on an oven rack at 180°C for 8–10 minutes.

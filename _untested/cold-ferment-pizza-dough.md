title: Cold Ferment Pizza Dough

Water should be cool, ideally below 16°C.

### Ingredients

#### Dough quantities

| Pizzas | Flour | Water | Yeast | Salt | Sugar | Olive oil |
|------:|------:|------:|------:|-----:|------:|----------:|
| 1     | 166g  | 103g  | 0.7g  | 4.1g | 3.2g  | 5.6g      |
| 2     | 332g  | 206g  | 1.35g | 8.2g | 6.5g  | 11.1g     |
| 3     | 498g  | 309g  | 2.0g  | 12.3g| 9.7g  | 16.7g     |
| 4     | 664g  | 412g  | 2.7g  | 16.4g| 12.9g | 22.3g     |

### Method

**Step 1**  
Mix the cool water with the yeast and set aside briefly.

**Step 2**  
Add the yeast water to the flour and mix until just combined.

**Step 3**  
Add the olive oil and mix until incorporated.

**Step 4**  
Add the salt and sugar, then mix by hand until fully combined.

**Step 5**  
Turn the dough out onto the counter and knead until smooth and elastic.

**Step 6**  
Place the dough into a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and rest briefly.

**Step 7**  
Divide the dough into 280g portions and shape into tight balls, sealing the seam underneath.

**Step 8**  
Place the dough balls into a lightly oiled container and cover.

**Step 9**  
Refrigerate for 2–4 days. Three days is ideal.

**Step 10**  
Bring to room temperature before stretching and baking.
